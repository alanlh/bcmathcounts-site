Art of Problem Solving

  • Alcumus: gain levels as you practice problems specifically tailored to your skill level.
  • MATHCOUNTS Trainer: work on real problems from past MATHCOUNTS contests. As you solve more problems, they increase in difficulty.
  • AoPS Videos: learn new concepts or concepts you are having trouble understanding.
  • For the Win: play against others in a speed-based game to solve problems. This is mainly useful for developing countdown skills.
  • Contests Collection: a massive collection of contests from the US, such as the AMCs, AIME, USAMO, and others.

Olympiad

IMOMath: a collection of some difficult practice materials suitable for hard contests and olympiads.

Olympiad Packets: a large collection of packets containing essential olympiad level knowledge and problems.
